Spiritual Overlook

🎭 Cultural Center

Modern Monastery with Ancient Soul

Perched above Gangtok city, Enchey Monastery offers a unique blend of spiritual serenity and urban accessibility. Built in 1909, this picturesque monastery is renowned for its spectacular panoramic views and vibrant Chaam dance performances that draw devotees and cultural enthusiasts from around the world.

🏙️ Discover Gangtok's Sacred & Cultural Treasures

Explore the vibrant attractions and spiritual sites surrounding Enchey Monastery in the heart of Sikkim's capital city

🕉️

Ganesh Tok

1 km away • Temple Views

A small but significant Ganesh temple offering spectacular panoramic views of Gangtok city and the surrounding hills. Perfect for a peaceful morning visit.

🐒

Hanuman Tok

5 km • Scenic Viewpoint

Another beloved temple and scenic viewpoint dedicated to Lord Hanuman, offering breathtaking vistas of the Himalayas and Gangtok valley below.

📚

Namgyal Institute of Tibetology

3 km • Cultural Museum

A renowned museum and research center dedicated to Tibetan culture, housing rare manuscripts, artifacts, and Buddhist art collections.

🏯

Do-Drul Chorten Stupa

3 km • Famous Stupa

One of Gangtok's most famous and significant Buddhist stupas, surrounded by 108 prayer wheels and offering a deeply spiritual atmosphere.

🌺

Flower Exhibition Centre

Near Ridge Park • Orchid Garden

A beautiful showcase of Sikkim's exotic orchids and native flowers, featuring vibrant displays of the region's incredible botanical diversity.

🛍️

MG Marg (Main Market)

2-3 km • Shopping & Dining

Gangtok's bustling pedestrian-only main street, perfect for shopping local handicrafts, enjoying delicious food, and experiencing the city's vibrant culture.

🚌 Transportation & Connectivity

🚍

SNT Bus Station (Deorali)

The main bus and shared jeep stand, about 3–4 km from Enchey Monastery. Shared jeeps and taxis are the most common transportation method.

🚕

Easy City Access

Being located in Gangtok, the monastery is easily accessible by foot, taxi, or local transport from anywhere in the city center.
