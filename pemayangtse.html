A Rich Historical Tapestry

The foundation of Pemayangtse Monastery was laid by Lhatsun Chempo, one of the three lamas who consecrated the first Chogyal (king) of Sikkim in 1642. Initially a small shrine, it was later enlarged and established as a monastery in 1705 by the third Chogyal, Chakdor Namgyal.
