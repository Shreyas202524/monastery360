👑 Royal Coronation

Where Sikkim's Royal Dynasty Began

The historic monastery near Yuksom, Sikkim's first capital, where in 1642 three wise lamas crowned Phuntsog Namgyal as the first Chogyal, establishing the Kingdom of Sikkim. This sacred site serves as the starting point for major Himalayan treks and preserves the profound legacy of Sikkim's royal heritage amidst breathtaking mountain vistas.
