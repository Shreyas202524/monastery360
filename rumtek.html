A Legacy of Faith

Rumtek Monastery, officially known as the Dharma Chakra Centre, stands as a testament to the resilience and preservation of Tibetan Buddhist traditions in exile.

Built as an exact replica of Tsurphu Monastery in Tibet, Rumtek serves as the main seat of the 16th Karmapa and continues to be a beacon of spiritual learning and practice.

The monastery houses precious relics, ancient manuscripts, and serves as an advanced center for Buddhist philosophy and meditation studies through the Nalanda Institute.

1959
16th Karmapa flees Tibet and arrives in Sikkim
1966
Rumtek Monastery rebuilt and consecrated
1980
Nalanda Institute established for Buddhist studies
Present
Continues as major pilgrimage destination

🏯 Explore East Sikkim's Sacred Capital

Discover the spiritual wonders and cultural treasures surrounding Rumtek Monastery in Gangtok and greater East Sikkim

🏛️

Ganesh Tok

Sacred Viewpoint • Lord Ganesha

A small temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha perched on a hilltop offering panoramic views of Gangtok city and the surrounding mountains. Perfect for sunrise prayers and meditation.

🐒

Hanuman Tok

11 km from Gangtok • Sacred Hill

An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman situated at 7,200 feet, offering spectacular views of the Himalayas and serving as a peaceful retreat for spiritual contemplation.

📚

Namgyal Institute

Gangtok • Tibetology Center

Premier research center for Tibetan and Himalayan studies, housing rare manuscripts, artifacts, and a world-class museum showcasing Buddhist culture and heritage.

⛩️

Do-Drul Chorten

Gangtok Center • Sacred Stupa

One of the most important stupas in Sikkim, built in 1946, containing complete sets of relics, holy books, and mantras. Surrounded by 108 prayer wheels for circumambulation.

🛍️

MG Marg

Gangtok Heart • Shopping & Culture

The vibrant pedestrian mall and cultural heart of Gangtok, featuring local handicrafts, traditional foods, and stunning mountain views, perfect for experiencing Sikkim's urban culture.

🌸

Flower Exhibition Centre

Ridge Park • Botanical Paradise

A stunning botanical garden showcasing Sikkim's rich flora including rare orchids, rhododendrons, and alpine flowers. A paradise for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.
