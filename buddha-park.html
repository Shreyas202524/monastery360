Sacred Creation and Spiritual Significance

Buddha Park of Ravangla represents a magnificent fusion of spiritual devotion, governmental vision, and community collaboration, creating a sacred space that promotes pilgrimage tourism while preserving Buddhist heritage in the Himalayas.

🙏 Blessed by His Holiness the 14th Dalai Lama

In 2013, this sacred complex was officially inaugurated by His Holiness the 14th Dalai Lama, blessing the site and establishing it as a major pilgrimage destination for Buddhist devotees worldwide.

The park commemorates the 2550th birth anniversary of Gautama Buddha, making it a historically significant spiritual landmark.

🏗️ Seven Years of Dedicated Construction

Built between 2006-2013 as a joint venture of the Government of Sikkim and local community, the park is situated within the larger religious complex of the ancient Rabong Gompa (Monastery), a centuries-old pilgrimage site.

Key Attractions and Sacred Spaces

The park offers a harmonious blend of spiritual architecture, natural beauty, and contemplative spaces designed to inspire inner peace and Buddhist contemplation.

🗿 130-Foot Majestic Buddha Statue

The towering statue of Lord Buddha in the Dharmachakra mudra is the park's centerpiece - an impressive feat of engineering and artistry that radiates peace and tranquility across the Himalayan landscape.

🏛️

Temple and Museum

Spacious temple and museum beneath the statue showcasing Buddha's life and teachings through intricate paintings and scriptures.

Ralong Monastery

6 km away

Important Kagyu monastery renowned for its sacred Chaam dances, ancient thangkas, and royal Sikkimese history. A spiritual gem showcasing authentic Tibetan Buddhist architecture.

⚡ Kagyu Lineage 🎭 Sacred Dances 👑 Royal Heritage

Bon Monastery (Kewzing)

~10 km away

Extremely rare Bon sect monastery - one of the few in Sikkim. Experience the ancient pre-Buddhist traditions with unique rituals, distinctive architecture, and mystical practices.

🌟 Rare Sect 🔮 Ancient Rituals 📿 Pre-Buddhist

Temi Tea Garden

15 km away

Sikkim's only tea estate offering spectacular views of rolling green hills. Witness tea processing, enjoy fresh Sikkim tea tasting, and capture Instagram-worthy plantation landscapes.
